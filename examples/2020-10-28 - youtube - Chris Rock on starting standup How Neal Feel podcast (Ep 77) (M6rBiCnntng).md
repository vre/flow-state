## Summary

**TL;DR**: Comedian accidentally discovered stand-up in 1980s while waiting for Eddie Murphy tickets. Passed first audition (1/40), dominated for three weeks, then bombed for three years.

## Arc

Left six-block ticket line on impulse for amateur night audition. Age 19-20, no experience—only person selected from 40 auditioners. "Miles Davis is so black, lightning bugs follow him into daytime" killed.

Three weeks of destroying sets → club considered SNL scouts. Then brought girlfriend, bombed continuously for three years to recover.

## Why the crash

Arrogance: "Once you cross that line, it's like fuck you." Youth: "There's no Justin Bieber of comedy"—audiences reject kids lacking life experience and point of view. Exception: Eddie Murphy.
