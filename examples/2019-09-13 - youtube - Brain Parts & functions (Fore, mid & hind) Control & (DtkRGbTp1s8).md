## Summary

**TL;DR**: Brain anatomy: forebrain (voluntary functions), midbrain (pupil reflex), hindbrain (involuntary processes).

## Forebrain
Cerebrum, thalamus, hypothalamus, pituitary. Controls: senses, cognition, memory, emotions, speech.

## Hindbrain
- Medulla: heartbeat, breathing, reflexes
- Cerebellum: balance, motor memory (why practiced skills become automatic; alcohol disrupts this)

## Midbrain
Pupillary light reflex.
