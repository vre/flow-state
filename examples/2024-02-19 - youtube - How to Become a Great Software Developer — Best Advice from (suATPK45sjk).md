## Summary

**TL;DR**: Master fundamentals over frameworks, avoid tech tribalism, balance work/life, aim higher.

**Skills**
- Precision: Character-level attention
- Fundamentals: Data structures, memory, concurrency
- People skills: Requirements/presentations > code time

**Mindset**
- "Developer" not "X developer"
- Adjacent knowledge compounds
- Curiosity > stale experience

**Career**
- Try diverse roles
- Sustainable pace > burnout
- Apply to unreachable jobs
