## Summary

**TL;DR**: Stable happiness comes from peaceful states of mind, not external circumstances. Practice meditation to cultivate inner peace.

## Core Insight

Happiness/unhappiness are mental states—their causes exist within the mind, not externally. Outsourcing happiness to circumstances makes it unstable. Peaceful mind = happy regardless of circumstances.

## Practice

**Meditation**: Mental action of concentrating on peaceful positive states. Can be formal (sitting) or integrated into daily life (patience while waiting).

**Breathing meditation**:
1. Exhale: release agitation (dark smoke)
2. Inhale: breathe clear light of inner peace
3. Let light fill body/mind
4. Carry peace into your day
